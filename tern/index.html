User Manual

📍 1. Setting Your Location

  • Tap Locate at the top to jump to your current GPS position.
  • Tap anywhere on the map to drop a new pin and start a checklist.

📝 2. Adding Records

  • When you drop a pin, a checklist form opens.
  • Fill in the Plot No. and Plot Notes at the top.
  • Type the Species, Count, and Remarks.
  • Tap + Add Species to continue adding birds to the same location.
  • Tap Save when finished. (Duplicates are merged automatically!)

✏️ 3. Editing & Deleting

  • On the Map: Tap any saved pin to view and edit the checklist.
  • In the Table: Use the + and - buttons to quickly adjust counts, or tap Edit/Delete.

💾 4. Exporting Data (CRITICAL)

  • Data is saved to your phone's offline memory.
  • At the end of your survey, you MUST tap Export to download your Excel file.
  • Once exported safely, tap Clear to wipe the map for your next survey.
